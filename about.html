OUR STORY

IT STARTED WITH ONE MISSED CELEBRATION.

GiftX began as a simple idea: to stop forgetting the people who matter. What started as a reminder tool quickly evolved into a smart gifting companion, combining event tracking, AI-powered gift suggestions, and personalized delivery options to help people celebrate across any distance.
